Notes:
  • All other characters (numbers, punctuation except period, symbols) are ignored
  • Case matters: uppercase letters map to black suits (♠♣), lowercase to red suits (♥♦)
  • This is Level None encoding - no encryption is applied
Encryption Levels:
  • Level 0 (None): Direct character-to-card mapping (shown above)
  • Level 1 (Simple Shift): All cards shift forward by the encryption key value (1-12). After King (13), wraps back to Ace (1)
  • Level 2 (Directional Shift): Odd-ranked cards (A, 3, 5, 7, 9, J, K) shift forward, even-ranked cards (2, 4, 6, 8, 10, Q) shift backward by the key value

Example: With Key=1 and Level 1, 'a' (A♥) becomes 2♥. With Key=1 and Level 2, 'a' (A♥, odd) becomes 2♥ (forward), but 'b' (2♥, even) becomes A♥ (backward).
